# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers of **Vidhi** pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and professional environment.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Threats of violence or encouragement of harmful behavior
- Any form of unethical use of legal content or misrepresentation of legal outcomes

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- Documentation contributions
- Any community channels related to this project

---

## Responsible Use (Important for Vidhi)
Vidhi is built to support legal research and document drafting workflows, but:

- Vidhi **does not replace legal professionals**
- Outputs must always be **reviewed and validated by a human**
- Contributors must avoid building features that encourage:
  - Unauthorized legal practice
  - Misleading legal advice
  - Illegal data scraping or violation of court portal terms
  - Citation fabrication or false precedent generation

We strongly encourage ethical development aligned with transparency and accountability.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. They may also temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior that is inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these community impact guidelines:

### 1. Correction
**Impact:** Minor inappropriate behavior  
**Action:** Private or public warning, request for correction.

### 2. Warning
**Impact:** Repeated or moderately harmful behavior  
**Action:** Formal warning, temporary restrictions.

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Impact:** Serious misconduct or repeated violations  
**Action:** Temporary ban from contribution and communication.

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Impact:** Harassment, threats, discriminatory behavior, or major violations  
**Action:** Permanent ban and removal from the community.
